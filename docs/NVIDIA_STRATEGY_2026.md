# NVIDIA NIM — Strategic Deployment Matrix (Audited March 2026)

This document defines the authoritative model-to-persona mapping for PartnerOS, based on the exhaustive topological audits of Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) sparsity, early-fusion multimodality, and test-time compute scaling.

---

## 1. 👑 THE PRINCIPAL TIER (Strategic Orchestration)
*Model Archetype: Sparse Frontier Generalist (Reinforcement Learning Optimized)*

### **Primary: DeepSeek-V3.1-Terminus**
- **Topology:** 671B Sparse (37B active).
- **Justification:** Frontier-class "System-2" reasoning. Specifically tuned for multi-hop deductive logic and reconciling conflicting agentic inputs.
- **Agent Assignment:** **The Manager Node.** 
- **Mandate:** Issues final strategic verdicts. Must handle complex "Partnership Exit" scenarios where multiple variables (IRR, DSCR, Commission) must be recalculated simultaneously.

---

## 2. 📊 THE UNDERWRITING ENCLAVE (Forensic Data)
*Model Archetype: High-Sparsity Coder MoE (Deterministic Schema Adherence)*

### **Primary: Qwen/Qwen3-Coder-480B-A35B-Instruct**
- **Topology:** 480B MoE (35B active).
- **Justification:** Leading 98.3% adherence to complex, nested JSON schemas. Models trained on code-generation corpora demonstrate superior mechanical accuracy in financial extraction.
- **Agent Assignment:** **The CFO Node.**
- **Mandate:** **Temperature = 0.** Zero-hallucination extraction of Rent Rolls and P&Ls. Deterministic math is non-negotiable.

---

## 3. 👁️ THE SENSORY LAYER (Multimodal Ingestion)
*Model Archetype: Early-Fusion Vision-Language Foundation*

### **Primary: Meta/Llama-4-Maverick-17B-128E-Instruct**
- **Topology:** 400B MoE (17B active).
- **Justification:** Early-fusion architecture tokenizes pixels and text jointly from layer one. This preserves high spatial fidelity required to parse bisected zoning boundaries and faded blueprints.
- **Agent Assignment:** **The Librarian Node (Visual/Classification).**
- **Secondary Ears:** Raw audio streams must pass through NVIDIA Riva (Canary/Parakeet) before text synthesis via Maverick.

---

## ⚡ THE FIREHOUSE TIER (High-Throughput Hunt)
*Model Archetype: Massive Context Anomaly (Linear-Time Sequence Processing)*

### **Primary: Meta/Llama-4-Scout-17B-16E-Instruct**
- **Topology:** 109B MoE.
- **Justification:** **10 Million Token Context Window.** Allows swallowing entire municipal CSV databases or 100+ property folders in a single prefill pass. 
- **Agent Assignment:** **AI Sourcer & Profiler.**
- **Metric:** Throughput of 2,600 tokens/sec enables "instant" RAG lookups across the entire Pinneo Brain.

---

## 🏎️ THE SUPPORT TIER (Routing & Support)
*Model Archetype: Dense Efficient Baseline*

### **Primary: NVIDIA/Mistral-Nemo-Minitron-8B-8K-Instruct**
- **Topology:** 8B Dense.
- **Justification:** Zero routing overhead ensures ultra-low latency. Outperforms all 8B-class models in instruction following for simple text routing.
- **Agent Assignment:** **Librarian (Text-only) & Initial Ingest Filter.**
